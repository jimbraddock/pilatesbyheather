HEATHER DARGE
Owner | Lead Trainer

My Pilates journey began shortly after my son was born in 2009. I tried different programs in order to shed pregnancy weight and get back in shape, but I was not seeing the results that I wanted. Eventually, I found pilates and everything changed. Soon after I began, I started seeing the results that I had been searching for; I have not looked back since! I truly fell in love with pilates during those early years. So much so that I wanted to train others, to share the amazing benefits that I had found. I felt confident that my training under one of the best instructors in the state would prepare me to teach those who were willing to commit to pilates the way I did.

I have now been a pilates trainer for over 10 years. My clients progression and improvement are paramount to me as an instructor. There is nothing more satisfying as an instructor than helping them reach their fitness goals and to exceed their expectations.
